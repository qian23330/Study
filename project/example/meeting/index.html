第二届基因组与转化医学国际研讨会

基因组学研究是揭示序列变异与基因功能、生命发育、疾病发生发展、人类表型的复杂调控关系的必然手段。近年来， 多组学（基因组、转录组、表观组、三维基因组、微生物组等）测序技术、AI技术和生物信息学、统计遗传学方法的 不断进步极大推动了功能基因组学研究的快速发展。转化医学是连接基因组学基础研究与临床应用的桥梁。 为进一步加强国内外基因组与转化医学研究界的交流与合作，推动生物医学发展和疾病药物研发，“第二届基因组与 转化医学国际研讨会”将邀请多位海内外知名学者和专家与会，大会将设置多个专题报告和墙报专栏。热忱欢迎相关 领域学者和学生与会，共商学科大计，共享盛举！

The 2nd International Symposium on Genomics and Translational Medicine

Genomics research is essential for revealing the complex regulatory relationships between sequence variations,human phenotypes, gene functiones, disease occurrence and development. In recent years, advancements in sequencing technologies for various omics and progress in bioinformatics, AI technology and statistical genetics methods have greatly facilitated the rapid development of functional genomics research. At the same time， translational medicine serves as a bridge connecting basic genomics research with clinical applications. In order to further enhance communication and collaboration between the domestic and international genomics and translational medicine research communities and promote the development of discovery of disease drugs, the "Second International Symposium on Genomics and Translational Medicine" will invite renowned scholars and experts from China and abroad. The conference will feature multiple thematic presentations with poster sessions and we warmly welcome scholars and students in relevant fields to participate, collaborate on interdisciplinary endeavors, and share advanced experiences in this grand event!
